# Project Brief: AIEN E-commerce Redesign

## 1. Project Overview
The AIEN redesign project aims to transform the existing e-commerce platform into a high-end, editorial-inspired shopping experience. The goal is to align the digital presence with the brand's sophisticated aesthetic, focusing on minimalism, high-quality imagery, and a seamless user journey.

## 2. Objectives
*   **Elevate Brand Perception:** Establish AIEN as a premium, modern design house through a sophisticated visual language.
*   **Improve User Engagement:** Create an immersive, editorial-style homepage that encourages exploration and discovery.
*   **Streamline Path to Purchase:** Optimize the product listing, detail, and cart pages to reduce friction and drive conversions.
*   **Ensure Visual Consistency:** Implement a robust design system (Aien) across all digital touchpoints.

## 3. Target Audience
*   **Modern Discerning Individuals:** Consumers who value architectural precision, refined materials, and minimalist aesthetics.
*   **Design-Conscious Shoppers:** Users looking for a curated experience that feels more like an editorial magazine than a traditional storefront.

## 4. Design Principles (Aien)
*   **Sophisticated Minimalism:** Clean layouts with generous whitespace to let products and imagery breathe.
*   **Editorial Typography:** Use of Noto Serif for a timeless, high-fashion feel combined with modern sans-serif accents.
*   **Monochromatic Palette:** A focused color scheme of deep blacks, soft whites, and subtle greys to maintain a premium atmosphere.
*   **Architectural Structure:** Grid-based layouts that emphasize precision and balance.

## 5. Key Features & Screens
*   **Homepage:** High-impact hero sections, curated collections, and integrated editorial content.
*   **Product Listing:** Clean grid layout with advanced filtering (size, color, price) and persistent navigation.
*   **Product Detail Page:** Multi-angle product imagery, detailed composition/care information, and "Complete the Look" recommendations.
*   **Shopping Bag:** A clear, summarized view of selections with integrated shipping and tax estimates for a transparent checkout start.

## 6. Success Metrics
*   Increase in average session duration.
*   Higher click-through rates on featured collections.
*   Reduction in cart abandonment rate.
*   Positive qualitative feedback on the new brand aesthetic.
